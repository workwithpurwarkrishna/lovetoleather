Data Protection Policy

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This page outlines what data we (Love to Leather) collect and how we use it.

1. General Information

No personal data is stored or tracked on this website. We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or user profiling systems.

2. Contact Form

When you use the contact form, your message is sent via Formspree and delivered directly to our email inbox. Your message is not stored on this website or in a database.

By submitting the form, you consent to us using your submitted information to respond to your request. We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about your stored data, its origin, and its purpose. You can also request the correction or deletion of your data. For such requests, please contact us at: info[at]lovetoleather.com

4. Data Security

We have taken technical and organizational measures to ensure your data is protected. Since this site does not store data, risk is minimized by design.
